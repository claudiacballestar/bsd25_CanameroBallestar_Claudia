## Unit Test

*Advantages of Unit Tests*
1. **The sooner, the better:** Unit tests are in the base of the Test Pyramid, and aims to find bugs in the very core of the code. 
Can **point out issues early on** as they are usually **carried out during the development**
*The sooner a problem is found, the better, and also, will be prevented from spreading.*

2. They **can be run quickly**: *Quick to run* and can point out issues early during the development phase.

3. They focus on the **smallest testable parts** of an application. They test a **single function** or **method** in **isolation**.
